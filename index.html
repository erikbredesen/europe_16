  • Reykjavik
    • Goðafoss has an interesting story surrounding it. Check out the wiki link for more. Syd snapped this great picture of one of many rainbows shining from the spray.


  • Akureyri
    • A bit south of Akureyri, we found the Mývatn Nature Baths. Like the Blue Lagoon, but at a much more reasonable price. Also, beer was brought to us, so we didn't have to brave the sub-40° F temps. We were treated to a pretty great view as the sun went down. (Full disclosure - this isn't our picture. Neither of us wanted to bring a phone into the lagoon)



    • During one of our day trips from Akureyri, we stopped at one of the bigger sulfer vents in the area. I have to admit I almost lost my lunch from catching too much of the vapor. Cool place, but kinda gross, in a way.
